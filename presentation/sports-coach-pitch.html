AI Sports Coach

AI-Powered Technique Analysis for Every Athlete

Real-time biomechanical feedback across 6 sports

The Problem

  • Recreational athletes lack affordable, data-driven technique feedback
  • Professional coaching is expensive — $50–150 / hour
  • Self-recorded videos are nearly impossible to analyze without expertise
  • No way to get objective, repeatable measurements of form

The Solution

  • Upload a video and get instant AI-powered coaching
  • Custom pose estimation extracts body keypoints frame-by-frame
  • Deterministic biomechanical rules evaluate form against sport-science thresholds
  • A–F scoring with specific, actionable improvement tips
